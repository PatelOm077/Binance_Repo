Features

Everything your hospital needs

From patient intake to discharge, MediCore covers every workflow so your team can focus on care.

Patient Records (EHR)

Centralized electronic health records with real-time updates, history tracking, and secure sharing across departments.

Appointment Scheduling

Smart scheduling engine with conflict detection, automated reminders via SMS & email, and patient self-booking portal.

Billing & Insurance

Automated invoicing, insurance claim submission, co-pay tracking, and full financial reporting dashboard.

Staff Management

Shift scheduling, role-based access control, performance tracking, and payroll integration for all hospital staff.

Bed Management

Real-time bed availability tracking, ward assignment, discharge planning, and capacity forecasting.

Customizable dashboards, KPI tracking, revenue analytics, and automated compliance reports for audits.
